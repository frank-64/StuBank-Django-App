FAQs

How to contact a Helper
1.Login (if you have not already). 2.Select “help” from the bar at the top. 3.Press "connect to helper" and a helper will be assigned to you.
What to do if you think someone has access to your account
1.Immidiately freeze your card. 2.Contact a Helper as soon as possible.
How do I transfer money to someone?
To transfer money to someone you must first add them as one of your payee's here. Once added, click the 'Make a Transfer' button on either the dashboard or your payee list and your payee will be visible in the dropdown of payees.
Where is my card information?
For security reasons StuBank does not display any card details on this site. You should already have your physical card.
Is StuBank a secure website?
At StuBank our security is our first priority, we achieve this through the use of 2-Factor-Authentication during user login, encryption of passwords using a SHA256 hash and use of TLS to ensure the encryption of data transmitted between you and our servers.
